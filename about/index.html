I am a designer who cares particularly about communicative, systematic, and iterative design. Collaboration is a must in the design process. It brings out the best, both in designers and their work.

I recently left my former job at TED after designing there for the last three and a half years. Who knows what comes next! I hope to find a small but multi-faceted team with whom to collaborate and work sleepless nights have design slumber parties.

I'm also a semi-retired competitive gamer who hails to you from the competitive Super Smash Brothers Melee community.

After stepping away from competing, I co-founded The New Meta, a panel about gender issues women face within competitive gaming spaces. As one of the eSports summit advisors at Game Developers Conference, I recently led a panel on growing the participation of women in eSports.

When possible, I try to tell my own story to spread awareness around and to promote empathy. I also blog specifically about gaming on my Tumblr.
