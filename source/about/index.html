Design

I am a designer who cares about communicative, systematic, and iterative design. Collaboration is a must in the design process. It brings out the best, both in designers and their work.

I currently work for YouTube on the Live team and live in San Francisco. Prior to that, I spent three and a half years designing for TED Talks in New York City.

Gaming

I'm also a semi-retired competitive gamer who hails to you from the competitive Super Smash Brothers Melee community.

After stepping away from competing, I co-founded The New Meta, a panel about gender issues women face within competitive gaming spaces. As one of the eSports summit advisors at Game Developers Conference, I recently led a panel on growing the participation of women in eSports.

When possible, I try to tell my own story to spread awareness around and to promote empathy.
